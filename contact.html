We are Carolyn Ayers and Alex Volpert.

We make paintings, magnets, pottery and zines.

Alex is influenced by geocities sites, byzantine icons, and 1960s era advertizements for kitchen implements.
Her creativity is stifled by the smell of most laundry detergents.
Carolyn is inspired by native insects and Rhombicosidodecahedrons.
